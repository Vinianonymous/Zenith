# A Simple Task Manager
## Why Zenith?
The meaning of Zenith is the highest point an astronomical body can take over our horizon. I believe that the pursuit of becoming the better self is one of the greatest human endeavours, by which I chose this name. The highest version of yourself will be made easier to reach through Zenith. 

## Terminology:
- Cycle: A period of Time established by the user, representing the minimal execution unit. Default is 30 Minutes.
- Metrics: Comparative Rulers by which  you judge your performance. The radar chart is a simple metric display, consisting of metrics you'll configure yourself such as:
	- Faith
	- Focus
	- Integrity
	- Speed
	- Communication
	- And etc. Even daily radar charts evaluating fields of life (Profession, Marriage, Etc) can be implemented.
